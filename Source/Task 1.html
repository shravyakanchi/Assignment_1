Everything about Ladhak!!!

History & About the city : Ladhak is situated in the northern part of India which is in Jammu & Kashmir . But recently it has been declared as a union territory. It is known for beautiful scenery and mountain ranges. It is one of the most visited tourist place in India. The largest towns in Ladhak are Leh & Kargil




Itinerary required for 5 Nights 6 Days:
  • Day - 1 : Reach Leh and get used to the climate for the day
  • Day - 2 : Explore the local Leh market and Leh palace
  • Day - 3 : Nubra Valley,Khardung La which is known as the world's highest motorable pass
  • Day - 4 : Visiting the Diskit Monestry and reach Pangong lake
  • Day - 5 : Exploring Pangong and start back to Leh
  • Day - 6 : Retrun to Delhi from Leh



Things required to carry:
  1. Two Thick Sweaters and woolen clothing
  2. Socks, gloves
  3. Boots or Shoes
  4. First aid and basic medicines
  5. Sunscreen (SPF 40 or above) and lipbalm
  6. Cold cream and other toiletries
  7. Mobile charger, earphones, powerbank
